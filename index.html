ABOUT US

The TIMBER VALLEY CAMPSITE at , Assam Lingzey, East Sikkim aims to be the premier camping option for all travellers visiting areas around East Sikkim by using the camp to offer a unique opportunity to explore the State`s natural beauty while experiencing alternative living in its simplest form. Our campsite is located in the bank of Takchangchu river of Assam Lingzey GPU East Sikkim. The Camp will provide a one-of-a-kind camping experience that offers travellers the opportunity to experience the Sikkim`s rain forests in a camp setting with a hostel like atmosphere. Presently, our camp site is in the introductory stage. Future plans include creating working relationships with other companies and furthering our knowledge of the surrounding hiking corridors that can facilitate the continued travel of our guests, such as Fishing, trekking to Khedi Lake etc. The greatest risks associated with our business today is transportation to and from the campsite valley, rainy weather conditions during a large portion of the year, and being off a main travel corridor . Our campsite area is at a distance of around 500 meters from roadside but our guests will experience a short trek and inbetween a Natural waterfall called maruni waterfall We feel we can overcome these risks because of traveller`s desire to learn more about the Natural Beauty of Our Sikkim.
